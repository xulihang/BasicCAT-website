Lezhin Comics is one of the most popular Korean webtoon platforms. While some titles are officially translated into other languages like English, many comics are still unavailable in languages such as Chinese, French, Thai, Vietnamese, and more.

If the webtoon you want to read hasn't been localized into your preferred language, [ImageTrans](/imagetrans/) Browser Extension can help.

Unlike built-in browser translators, ImageTrans translates the text inside comic images. It detects dialogue in speech bubbles, translates it into your chosen language, and replaces the original text directly on the page, making webtoons much easier to read.

## Works with AI Translation

ImageTrans supports multiple translation engines, including modern AI models.

If you're reading story-heavy webtoons, AI translation usually produces much more natural dialogue than traditional machine translation.

You can even enable visual context, allowing vision-language models to understand the artwork when translating difficult scenes.

## More Than Just Lezhin Comics

The extension also works on many other websites where text is embedded inside images, including:

* Korean webtoon platforms
* Japanese manga websites
* Chinese manhua sites
* Comic readers
* Image galleries
* Social media posts
* Scanned books and magazines

If the content is an image, ImageTrans can usually translate it.

## Available on Major Browsers

ImageTrans is available for:

* Chrome
* Microsoft Edge
* Firefox (including Firefox Android)
* Kiwi Browser on Android
* Orion Browser on iPhone and iPad

This means you can read translated comics on both desktop and mobile devices.

## Give It a Try

If you're looking for a Lezhin Comics translation browser extension, ImageTrans offers an easy way to read Korean webtoons without leaving your browser.

Instead of taking screenshots, copying text into translators, or using OCR apps, you can translate comic pages with a single click and focus on enjoying the story.
